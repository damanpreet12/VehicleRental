Various damage options:
Level 0: Minor damage typically means scratches, scrapes or dings. For example, a cracked headlight or small dent in your hood.
Level 1: Moderate damage usually means large dents in the hood, fender or door of your car.
Level 2: If the doors won't open, or if airbags have deployed, you likely have a level2 amount of damage to your car.
Major : Severe damage is just like it sounds: very heavy damage. This includes broken axles and bent or twisted frames. In a severe damage situation, air bags have almost always deployed. Examples include if your car has rolled over, or a complete side of the car is damaged.
