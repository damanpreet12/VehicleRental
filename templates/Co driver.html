WHY
If you are going on a long drive and expect your friend to drive, do add them as a co-driver here. This is mandatory and ensures that in case of any damage while your friend is driving, liability is limited to Rs. 10000(under our usual conditions).
THE PROCESS
If your friend is not a VRental user already, we will ask them to upload their DL and will then review it. If your friend is already a VRental user then their DL has most likely already been approved.
